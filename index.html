Gillette

The Best a Woman Can Get

Role Art Direction & Copy
Type Integrated Campaign
Year 2025

A campaign reimagining Gillette's iconic tagline for the modern woman. We flipped the script on traditional razor advertising — no more blue razors and clinical bathrooms. Instead: pink bubbles, bold confidence, and the unapologetic joy of self-care.

The campaign spans print, social, and digital, with a bathroom scene that turns an everyday ritual into a moment of empowerment.

About Us

The duo behind Dreamhouse

Naomi

Art Director

Sees the world in mood boards and color palettes. Turns concepts into visuals that make you feel something. Currently obsessed with Y2K aesthetics and experimental typography.

Jana

Copywriter

Finds the perfect words for imperfect moments. Believes every brand has a story worth telling — and that story should sound like a conversation, not a press release.

Together

We're two advertising students who believe the best work happens when art and words collide. This dreamhouse is our playground — a space where campaigns live inside rooms, and every corner tells a story.

We're looking for internships where we can bring this energy to real brands. If you like what you see, let's talk.

Your Vibe, Your Soundtrack

Role Art Direction & Copy
Type Digital Campaign
